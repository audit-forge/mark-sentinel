# AI Usage Policy

## Purpose
Define approved AI use inside the business and reduce risk from unsafe or unauthorized usage.

## Approved tools
- Hash-AI (approved internal deployment)
- Any explicitly approved provider/model listed by the owner

## Prohibited uses
- Entering secrets, production credentials, PHI, PCI, or other regulated data into unapproved AI systems
- Using AI output without human review for high-impact decisions
- Installing unapproved AI plugins, agents, or models

## Data handling
- Sensitive data must be minimized before submission to AI systems
- Production secrets must remain in environment variables or secret stores, never in repo files

## Ownership
- System owner: [Your Name / Organization]
- Security/review owner: designated maintainer or security lead

## Review cadence
- Review every 12 months or after any major deployment change
